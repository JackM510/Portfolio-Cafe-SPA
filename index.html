ABOUT US

Speciality Coffee

We’re passionate about serving exceptional coffee all day long, using beans that are sustainably sourced right here in Australia.

Our house blend, expertly roasted to perfection, is crafted to complement any coffee style or milk preference, delivering rich, consistent flavour in every cup. We take pride in sourcing premium beans from sustainable growers across Australia, ensuring every sip celebrates quality and care. Whichever way you take your coffee, we’re here to make it just the way you like it.

Fresh Whole Foods

Our menu is crafted with the freshest whole food ingredients sourced locally in the region.

Every dish is prepared from scratch in-house, celebrating the natural goodness of whole foods, and our baked goods are homemade to ensure every bite is as wholesome as it is delicious.

Seaside Serenity

Just a short stroll from cozy corner beach, we’re your go-to spot for moments that matter.

Whether you’re popping in for a coffee to-go, meeting up with friends for a sit-down, or treating yourself to some quiet time, we’re here to create the perfect escape for you.
